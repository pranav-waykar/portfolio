I'm a UX Practitioner based in Pune, India

Hello there! I'm Pranav Waykar, a Senior User Experience Designer based in Pune, India. I am currently working on designing two digital products under the ZAIDYN platform at ZS Associates.

I'm a designer who's not afraid to get my hands dirty with code. With a background in computer engineering, I bring a technical perspective to my design work. This helps me bridge the gap between design and development, ensuring highly usable products that can be developed within real-world constraints.

While working at Infosys Limited, I had the privilege of participating in the Strategic Design Program at the Center of Complexity at Rhode Island School of Design (RISD). The program focused on approaching complex systemic problems in a structured manner, and designing interventions that have a measurable impact. This unique skillset has proven invaluable in my work as a UX Practitioner, allowing me to navigate challenging problem spaces where information is ambiguous or incomplete.

When I am not geeking out over UX, I like to take long walks on the nearby hills and indulge in my passion for reading.
